❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Which build tool is the fastest?

ESBuild and SWC are among the fastest due to their use of Go and Rust, respectively, but tools like Vite provide a more complete developer experience.

Is Vite better than Webpack?

Vite offers faster development builds, while Webpack provides more configurability and a mature plugin ecosystem.

Should I replace Babel with SWC?

SWC is significantly faster, but Babel still has a larger plugin ecosystem.

What matters more: speed or ecosystem?

It depends on your project. Speed improves developer productivity, but ecosystem maturity ensures long-term stability.
